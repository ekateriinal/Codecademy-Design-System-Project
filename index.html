#f8f8ff

In a RGB color space, hex #f8f8ff (also known as Ghost white) is composed of 97.3% red, 97.3% green and 100% blue. Whereas in a CMYK color space, it is composed of 2.7% cyan, 2.7% magenta, 0% yellow and 0% black.

#e6e6fa

In a RGB color space, hex #e6e6fa (also known as Lavender, Lavender mist) is composed of 90.2% red, 90.2% green and 98% blue. Whereas in a CMYK color space, it is composed of 8% cyan, 8% magenta, 0% yellow and 2% black.

#ffff66

In a RGB color space, hex #ffff66 (also known as Unmellow Yellow) is composed of 100% red, 100% green and 40% blue. Whereas in a CMYK color space, it is composed of 0% cyan, 0% magenta, 60% yellow and 0% black.
